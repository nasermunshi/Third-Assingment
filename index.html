The game is typically played on a rectangular rink with goals at either end. Each

How many players are on a standard ice hockey team?

A standard ice hockey team has six players on the ice at a time, including one goaltender.

What is the objective of a hockey game?

The primary objective in hockey is to score more goals than the opposing team by shooting the puck into their net.

What is the penalty box in hockey?

The penalty box is an area where players serve time for infractions. Teams play with fewer players when a member is in the penalty box due to penalties.

What is the blue line in hockey for?

The blue lines on the hockey rink define the offensive, defensive, and neutral zones. Players must stay onside, meaning they cannot cross the opponent's blue line before the puck does during an attack.

What is a hat trick in hockey?

A hat trick is when a player scores three goals in a single game. Fans sometimes throw hats onto the ice to celebrate this achievement.

What is the Zamboni used for in hockey?

The Zamboni is a machine used to resurface the ice between periods, ensuring a smooth playing surface for the players.
